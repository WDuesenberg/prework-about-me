Formed in 1983 on the campus of the University of Vermont in Burlington, Phish has been around a minute! The band gained popularity and notoriety through their live performances, where they are particularly skilled with musical improvisation which is commonly known and referred to as "Jams". Besides music, Phish may be equally famous for their extremely dedicated phanbase. Which is arguably one of the most diehard and infamous in American music. Since their formation, Phish has released 13 studio albums and has managed to record and catalog almost every live performance in the bands existence(click the link below to visit live phish show catalog).
